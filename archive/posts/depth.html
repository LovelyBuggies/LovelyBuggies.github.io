What is the default order?

The posts are in order of dates by default.

What does specialty mean in my posts?

1: Fundamental and extensions, students should learn.
2: Core concepts and idea, researchers in this area should know.
3: Recent progress of core concepts, people who are doing similar projects would like to know.
4: Placeholder.
5: Specialized stuff, some detailed proofs of the paper I found interesting.

How long does it take to read each post?

The reading time varies. I use a lot of math in my writing, so those more comfortable with math might find it easier to read through. Besides, I prefer not to have an exact reading time to spoil the fun of exploring more deeply.
