What is the default order?

The posts are in order of dates by default.

What does specialty mean in my posts?

1: Fundamentals and extensions in a field, every CS student should learn.
2: Core concepts and idea in a subfield, researchers in the same field should know.
3: Context or progress of core concepts, researchers in the same field want to skim.
4: Details of a core specialization, research peers may want to dive into.
5: Details of an interesting work, research peers may want to skim.

Note that the specialty does not necessarily mean difficulty.

How long does it take to read each post?

The reading time varies. I use a lot of math in my writing, so those more comfortable with math might find it easier to read through, and I prefer not to have an exact reading time to spoil the fun of thinking.

Who are the authors?

Most of the posts are written by me. I welcome any suggestions. If you want to cooperate on or cite these posts, please email me.
